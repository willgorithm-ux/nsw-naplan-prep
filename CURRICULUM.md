## NAPLAN Assessment Framework Alignment

### Reading (Domain)
- Tests comprehension of texts
- Emphasis on finding literal and implied meaning
- Year 3 texts are shorter with predictable structures
- Questions focus on vocabulary, main ideas, inference

### Numeracy (Domain)
- Tests number fluency, problem-solving, reasoning
- Year 3 focus: whole numbers to 100, addition/subtraction, fractions, patterns
- Multi-step word problems included

### Language Conventions (Domain)
- Grammar: sentence structure, verb tense, pronouns
- Punctuation: capitals, full stops, question marks
- Spelling: phonetically regular and irregular words, high-frequency words

### Writing (Domain)
- Year 3: paper-based writing tasks
- Tests ability to compose narratives, descriptions, procedural texts
- Focus on sentence construction, capitalization, basic punctuation
- Tasks generate approximately 150-300 words

## Learning Progression Model

The app implements a **4-level difficulty scale** aligned to Year 3 content:

### Level 1 (Foundation)
- Basic sight words and decoding
- Single-digit addition/subtraction
- Simple sentences with correct punctuation
- Single-word spelling

### Level 2 (Developing)
- Compound sight words and phrases
- Two-digit addition/subtraction
- Multi-clause sentences
- CVC pattern spelling

### Level 3 (Proficient)
- Context-dependent vocabulary
- Two-step problems
- Complex sentences with variety
- Multi-syllable spelling patterns

### Level 4 (Extending)
- Specialized vocabulary (domain-specific)
- Multi-step reasoning problems
- Extended text composition
- Irregular spelling patterns

## Mastery Criteria (3-Correct System)

- **Unseen**: Not yet attempted
- **Learning**: Attempted, some errors, streak < 3
- **Mastered**: 3 correct in a row on same subskill

Upon mastery:
- Difficulty increases +1 (capped at 5)
- Bonus gems awarded (+10)
- Item moves to review queue for later interleaving

---

## Assessment & Reporting

Progress tracked across:
- **Subskill mastery %**: Total mastered / total subskills
- **Daily streak**: Consecutive days of engagement
- **Session gems**: Cumulative rewards (25 gems per correct answer)
- **Difficulty growth**: Current vs. starting difficulty per subskill
